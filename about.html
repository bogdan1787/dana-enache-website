Dana Enache

Dana Enache is the author of THE DREAM, ON VACATION, and EVOLUTION — chilling tales that will keep you up at night. She lives in Ireland with her husband, two kids, and a suspiciously long dog. Dana published some poems in her youth and swears that was just a phase.
