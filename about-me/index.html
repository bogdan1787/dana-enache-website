About Dana Enache

The woman behind the nightmares.

Dana Enache

Dana Enache is the author of THE DREAM, ON VACATION, and EVOLUTION — psychological horror novels that blur the line between what’s real and what hides in the dark corners of the mind.

She writes horror that gets under your skin slowly: no jump-scares, just dread. The kind that follows you to bed. Her stories draw on psychological tension, the uncanny, and the terrifying ordinariness of everyday life turned wrong — the suspicion that something familiar is not quite right anymore.

She lives in Ireland with her husband, two kids, and a suspiciously long dog. Dana published some poems in her youth and swears that was just a phase — though she admits the poems were also unsettling.
